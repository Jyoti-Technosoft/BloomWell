## ðŸ“š **Training Modules**

### **Module 1: HIPAA Fundamentals**
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Topics**:
  - What is HIPAA?
  - Protected Health Information (PHI)
  - Covered Entities and Business Associates
  - HIPAA Privacy Rule
  - HIPAA Security Rule
  - Breach Notification Rule
- **Assessment**: 20-question quiz (80% passing grade)

### **Module 2: Privacy Rule Compliance**
- **Duration**: 1.5 hours
- **Topics**:
  - Patient rights under HIPAA
  - Uses and disclosures of PHI
  - Minimum necessary standard
  - Business Associate Agreements
  - Patient consent and authorization
- **Assessment**: 15-question quiz

### **Module 3: Security Rule Implementation**
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Topics**:
  - Administrative safeguards
  - Physical safeguards
  - Technical safeguards
  - Access controls and audit logs
  - Encryption and data protection
  - Incident response procedures
- **Assessment**: 25-question quiz

### **Module 4: BloomWell Specific Policies**
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Topics**:
  - BloomWell privacy policy
  - Data handling procedures
  - Security incident reporting
  - Patient request procedures
  - System access protocols
- **Assessment**: 10-question quiz
