# HIPAA Training Program

## BloomWell Healthcare HIPAA Compliance Training

### Training Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) training program for all BloomWell Healthcare employees, contractors, and business associates.

### Training Objectives

- Ensure understanding of HIPAA Privacy and Security Rules
- Protect Protected Health Information (PHI)
- Maintain compliance with federal regulations
- Prevent data breaches and unauthorized disclosures

### Training Modules

#### Module 1: HIPAA Fundamentals
- Overview of HIPAA legislation
- Privacy Rule requirements
- Security Rule requirements
- Enforcement and penalties

#### Module 2: Protected Health Information (PHI)
- Definition of PHI
- Identifying sensitive data
- Proper handling procedures
- Data classification

#### Module 3: Technical Safeguards
- Access controls and authentication
- Encryption and data protection
- Audit logging and monitoring
- Secure communication protocols

#### Module 4: Administrative Safeguards
- Privacy policies and procedures
- Employee training requirements
- Incident response procedures
- Business associate agreements

#### Module 5: Breach Notification
- Identifying breaches
- Notification requirements
- Documentation procedures
- Mitigation strategies

### Training Schedule

- **New Employees**: Within 30 days of hire
- **Annual Refresher**: All staff annually
- **Policy Updates**: Within 15 days of changes
- **Incident Response**: Immediate training after incidents

### Compliance Requirements

All employees must:
- Complete initial training
- Pass competency assessment (80% or higher)
- Sign HIPAA compliance agreement
- Participate in ongoing education

### Monitoring and Enforcement

- Training completion tracking
- Regular compliance audits
- Performance evaluations
- Disciplinary actions for violations
